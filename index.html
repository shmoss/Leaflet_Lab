Homicides in the Rustbelt

Which Midwestern cities have the highest homicide rates?

Beginning in the 1970's, liberalized trade policies have drained metropolitan regions of their manufacturing base, creating urban blight across the Midwest. Many of these cities has seen a corresponding increase in homicides.

This interactive map depicts yearly homicide totals (2001-2013) for the 15 largest Midwestern cities. Homicides are represented in two different ways: a rate per 100,000 people and raw totals.

This data was obtained from City Data and is public domain.
